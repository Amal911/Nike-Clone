Black and Anthracite

Step into the ISPA Link Axis, a continuation of our journey to push the needle of footwear design. "Every part can be recycled", says the ISPA team. "It represents progress over perfection—creating a product with a second life in mind". Guided by the principles of modularity and waste reduction, the design uses interlocking components, zero glue and as few materials as possible. This results in a new look and feel, too. One thing remains the same: Nike Air cushioning adds responsiveness and the perfect amount of bounce. When you've finished wearing these shoes, simply drop them off at a participating Nike store for recycling. It's a reminder of our promise: everlasting progress towards the forever shoe. Imagine what comes next.
